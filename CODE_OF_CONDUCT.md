# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the BRIDGE project a respectful
and constructive experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- being respectful and constructive
- focusing on technical substance rather than personal attacks
- welcoming questions and clarifications
- assuming good intent while still giving honest feedback

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks or insulting language
- deliberate disruption of discussions or review processes
- publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public interactions
when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, or
other interactions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
